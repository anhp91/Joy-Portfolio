Whaa.Life

Whaa connects users and the community to engage with corporation/brands in charity projects and community activities.

Released in Dec, 2016 - Visit WhaaLife

Project Overview

Whaa Life is an social enterise platform, belongs to Whaa Media (my very first startup, together with Whaa Audio and Whaa TV). Whaa Life connects users and the community to engage with corporation/brands in charity projects, community activities, causes we all care about. Whaa takes action by giving vaccines and medical program for kids, education, food for people and environment.

Whaa does not raise fund directly from our partners to ensure transparency. All activities will be funded by the corporate/brands, and Whaa will act as a mediate platform.

We work with the most trustworthy corporate/brands and NGOs in Vietnam whom contribute more community activities.

Discovery

In this project, I worked closely with the founder to define high-level requirements and business strategy from scratch.

This discovery was conducted by UX & Market analytics. The goal of feature and functional discovery is to collect all requirements for the product, research the current state of a market to find the competitors, and establish a technological stack for it.
Product discovery includes:
+ Analyze user expectations
+ Clearly define business objectives
+ Create a competitive comparison

Prototype & Iterate

Once we had synthesized our research it was time to start developing our developing wireframes and prototyping. My team collected all requirements from stakeholders. Then we conducted an analysis of premium media brand - Unilever, the technologies they use, and the solutions they provide for their customers. The team analysed both benefits and downsides of different solutions. Based on this information, the team proposed a set of features that should be implemented in a product. It helps to create a better stream-lined and strategic product.

The product is iterated few times after testing with targeted clients. The iterative prototyping doesn't only allow us to validate our ideas, but also catch technical hurdles early in the process, but also to engaged user experiences and adapt with real content strategy that agencies provide.

I worked closely with other developers to build the front-end and design system for this platform.

Key Result

WhaaLife just released in Dec 2016 and get accepted into the program of FbStart that FB supports for new startups. We also got some offers and parnership contract with Unilever and Japanese media agencies.

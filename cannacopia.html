Cannacopia

Quickly discover which marijuana strains provide the effects you desire
The app was developed in 2015. Download app on

App Store or Google Play

Project Brief

With over 7500 different marijuana strains to choose from, the Cannacopia app solves the confusion of what cannabis strains to purchase and where to purchase them.

Cannacopia is easy and fun to use and unlike any other marijuana discovery app available today. There are 4 touch-screen buttons representing MOOD, PHYSICAL, MEDICAL and TASTE that you slide across users' screen to determine which cannabis strain will give users their desired effect. Cannacopia then connects users with near-by licensed medical marijuana dispensaries or retail outlets where marijuana use is legal.

I worked with another designer to develop this interesting project.

Discover & Define

Client came to us with the requirement "create a fun visual search with 4 categories and 12 key attributes" ?! Oops, so my work started by learning a very new user segment, the strain smokers.
+ How do they look for strains?
+ What are important factors?
+ How many types of strains and their effects?
+ When do they look for strains? Why?
...
I never use weeds before, so this is totally a new market. After many hours of research, discussions and sketches with engineerong team, We were able to reform the problems and limitations of the visual search.

Design & Development

Once we had synthesized our research it was time to start developing wireframes and algorithm strategy, create rapid prototyping & storyboard to test workflow & level of accuracy with client's strain database.

While color palette and app icon were provided by client, we explored with some concepts around the existing color scheme to be more inline with gradient colors, similar to illusions people see when getting high.


Outcome

The business has been pretty successful and keep expanding in the U.S market.

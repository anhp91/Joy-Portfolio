Project Brief

The goal of this projects is to create a new collaborative platform to help companies improve their supply chain performance.

Instead of the traditional consulting model, the new platform will focus on helping companies to develop their own internal capabilities to implement supply chain improvement projects with the help of a global team of experts and coaches.

It will support companies as they improve the maturity of their supply chain management processes and internal capabilities, offering tools to support consulting projects, training, coaching, and process improvement project execution.

Discovery

The challenge of this project is about information architecture to control huge database with complicated user roles and business models. Further more, all database and documents that client provided team are in Chinese, so it takes quite a lot of time for team to fully understand it.

Design & Development

Once we had synthesized our research it was time to start developing wireframes and rapid prototyping. We focus on the content and the overall goal of each page without getting bogged down in the details. The wireframes functioned as a sounding board for the project – allowing us to discuss concepts and quickly make revisions when needed.

As we iterated, we explored clever ways to integrate events and online trainning courses. The iterative prototyping allowed us to validate our ideas discover new ideas, and catch technical hurdles early in the process.
